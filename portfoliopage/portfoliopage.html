About Me

Educated in engineering (because solving math problems is oddly satisfying), I ventured into the corporate jungle where I managed more spreadsheets than emotions. Joined a research team investigating how the environment hijacks our decision-making (plot twist: it does). Helped future doctors think critically - ideally before their second cup of coffee and definitely before diagnosing themselves. With a background in Montessori leadership and a deep interest in human development, I dream in developmental principles and neural pathways - especially during the tender beginnings of childhood and the quiet rewiring of elderhood..
